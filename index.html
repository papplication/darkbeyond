Dark Beyond

It's setting place in the epicenter of gravity field where you have to sustain energy level by destroying circles and making combos. Like all great puzzles, Dark Beyond will keep you entertained for just a moment, or will keep you engaged for hours of fun.


NEW EXPERIENCE

On every level you have to complete different objectives. Destroy and swap circles, make combos or stay energized before the time runs out.

INNOVATIVE GAMEPLAY

Your goal in each level is to clear the circles and substain energy level by make combos, and use special game mechanism.

ENDLESS PROGRESSION

Never-ending fun with thousands of unique levels and more to come. Player level progression and leaderboards keep you playing.
